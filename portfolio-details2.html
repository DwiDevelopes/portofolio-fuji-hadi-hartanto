Program Madinah information

    The International Cultural Festival is a festival attended by 560 participants representing 80 countries. This festival was held from February 27 to March 11 in a giant tent located in the UIM complex. This annual cultural event was opened by the Governor of Madinah Prince Faisal bin Salman bin Abdul Aziz, and closed by the Chancellor of UIM Dr Hatem bin Hassan al-Marzouqi, with the distribution of various prizes and awards to participants for the best booth category and lucky visitors.
